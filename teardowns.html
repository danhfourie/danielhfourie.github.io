Teardowns

In order to pick up more domain knowledge on how-things-are-manufactured-in-real-life, I've taken to more frequently tearing down consumer electronics devices. It's fun to try to decipher how the architects thought. Why this? Why that? Clumsy friends/siblings and e-waste disposals are great sources of fodder! I'll hopefully be updating this page with the most interesting features I find.
