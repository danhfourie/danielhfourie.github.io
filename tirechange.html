A Better Tire Changing Experience

In most cases, when someone gets a flat tire, they call up the AAA and wait the hour it takes for the tow truck to arrive and help them. Most people don't bother changing the tire themselves because the process involves confusing instructions, high forces, and getting dirty. In order to make the process more attractive, we developed a brand new wrench, interactive video instructions, and an informational floormat.


The multifunction wrench combines two tools into one with a pivoted, locking joint. It provides a long lever arm to easily break off tight lug nuts, a dog leg to crank up the jack, and a balanced socket to quickly spin off nuts. I did the product design and fabrication of the wrench.
